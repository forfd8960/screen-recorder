# /speckit.clarify Command Template

Purpose: Resolve high-impact requirement ambiguities in the active spec before planning and implementation.

## Required Steps

1. Load active feature spec and perform ambiguity scan.
2. Prioritize unresolved items by impact and uncertainty.
3. Ask targeted clarification questions (bounded count).
4. Integrate accepted answers directly into the correct spec sections.
5. Re-validate consistency and remove contradicted statements.

## Validation Rules

- Clarification questions are limited and high-impact.
- Every accepted answer is reflected in spec updates.
- Updated requirements remain testable and measurable.
- No contradictory legacy statements remain after integration.

## Scope and Constraints

- Do not over-ask low-value questions.
- Do not defer critical ambiguity without explicit rationale.
- Keep edits localized and traceable.
